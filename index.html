OUR INVESTMENT STRATEGY

Selection Criteria

We target private companies with clear IPO signals, recent funding rounds, validated valuations, and strong growth trajectories. Our proprietary scoring model identifies the top 20 opportunities.

Governance

Rigorous investment committee oversight with quarterly rebalancing. Strict 10% position limits ensure diversification. Independent valuation methodology.

Methodology

NAV calculated using 50% secondary market pricing, 30% last-round valuation, and 20% VC model. Continuous monitoring of IPO pipelines and private-market activity.
